Visit Lindsay

Enjoy some old buildings and some open space. It's slow but that's good for some people. It's peaceful at times.

These are the top 3 things that you can do in Lindsay

Go Birding

Hit up Ken Reid Conservation Area. There are forests and marshlands that are home to many different species!

Go Fishing

If you like largemouth bass and muskies, this is the place to be. Put a canoe down the Scugog River and catch the big one!

Go Bowling

Lindsay is home to 5 pin bowling. Enjoy the extra challenge of less targets and smaller balls!
